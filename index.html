Why Trees Matter

Trees are vital to our ecosystem and provide countless benefits:

  • Produce oxygen and absorb carbon dioxide
  • Regulate water cycles and prevent soil erosion
  • Provide habitat for wildlife
  • Cool urban environments
  • Improve mental and physical health
